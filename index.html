Research & Projects

Assessing Green Bond Yield Differences

Analyzed the “greenium” using a matching method pairing green and conventional bonds based on maturity, credit rating, and issuer profile to isolate yield differences, minimizing omitted variable bias. Report (PDF) GitHub

Sunlight Synchronisation: DST & Emissions

Studied the impact of Daylight Saving Time on CO₂ emissions and electricity consumption in Australia's electricity market using a Difference-in-Differences-in-Differences framework to control for state-specific shocks. Report (PDF) GitHub

Volatility & Risk Modeling in Financial Econometrics

Implemented ARCH/GARCH and leverage-augmented models via hand-written QMLE on S&P 500 returns to compare distributional assumptions, enhancing VaR forecasting. Applied PCA for factor extraction and continuous-time structural models to analyze market microstructure noise.

Extreme Risk Analysis of FX Markets

Modeled tail risk in the USD–EUR exchange rate (2004–2023) using threshold exceedances and Generalised Pareto distributions, switching to Hill estimator for efficiency, and adjusting confidence intervals for serial dependence, revealing significant intra-day volatility risks.

Multivariate Time Series & Climatic Impacts

Examined how extreme weather events affect insurance prices by estimating VAR models with climate risk indicators and disaster cost indices, using Granger causality and impulse response functions, and conducting robustness checks with CPI and PPI metrics.

Scoring & Default Prediction

Compared logistic regression, Lasso/Ridge penalized methods, and ensemble classifiers (AdaBoost, LogitBoost) for credit default scoring, validating models via hold-out and K-fold cross-validation to ensure robust predictive performance.
